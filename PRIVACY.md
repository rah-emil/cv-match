# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26.06.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CV Match handles user data.

## Overview

CV Match is a browser extension that helps users create, improve, and tailor CVs, resumes, cover letters, and job application materials.

The extension is designed to work locally in the user’s browser. We do not operate our own backend for storing, processing, or analyzing user CVs, job descriptions, generated documents, API keys, or other personal data.

## Data You May Provide

Depending on how you use the extension, you may enter or store the following types of data:

* CV, resume, or professional profile information
* Contact details
* Employment history
* Education, skills, achievements, and career information
* Job descriptions and vacancy details
* Cover letters and generated application materials
* User settings
* API keys or access credentials for third-party AI providers selected by the user

## Local Storage

Data entered into the extension is stored locally in your browser, using browser storage mechanisms such as local extension storage.

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, rent, or share this data on our own servers.

You are responsible for managing and deleting locally stored data on your device. You can remove locally stored extension data by clearing the extension’s storage in your browser settings or by uninstalling the extension.

## Third-Party AI Providers

The extension may allow you to connect your own account and API key from third-party AI providers, such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or other supported providers.

When you choose to use an external AI provider, the content required to perform the requested action may be sent directly from your browser to the selected provider using your own API key and account access.

This may include CV text, job descriptions, cover letters, prompts, generated content, and related application materials.

We do not control how third-party AI providers process, store, retain, or use data submitted to their services. Your use of those providers is governed by their own terms, privacy policies, data processing terms, and account settings.

Before using any third-party AI provider, you should review that provider’s privacy policy and data usage terms.

## API Keys

If you enter an API key into the extension, it is stored locally in your browser.

We do not receive, collect, store, or have access to your API keys.

You are responsible for protecting your own API keys and for managing usage, billing, permissions, and security settings in your third-party AI provider account.

## No Server-Side Data Collection

We do not operate a server-side database that stores your CVs, job descriptions, generated documents, API keys, or personal application materials.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, resale, or training our own AI models.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.

Data may be shared only when you intentionally use a third-party AI provider through the extension, in which case the relevant content is sent to the provider selected by you for the purpose of generating or improving application materials.

## Analytics and Tracking

CV Match does not use hidden tracking, advertising identifiers, or profiling technologies.

If analytics are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to clearly describe what is collected and why.

## Data Deletion

Because data is stored locally in your browser and not on our servers, we cannot remotely access, export, or delete your data.

You can delete your data by:

* Removing saved data inside the extension, if this feature is available
* Clearing the extension’s local storage in your browser
* Uninstalling the extension

You can also revoke or delete API keys through your third-party AI provider account.

## Security

We design the extension to minimize data collection and keep user-controlled data local whenever possible.

However, no browser extension, local storage mechanism, or third-party service can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should avoid entering information that you do not want to process through your selected AI provider.

## Children’s Privacy

CV Match is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
